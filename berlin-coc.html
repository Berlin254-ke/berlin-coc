Biography

The Story Behind
the Studio

Berlin C.O.C. was born from a singular obsession: creating digital experiences that don't just look exceptional — they perform. Founded on the belief that design and technology are not separate disciplines but two sides of the same coin.

With over 5 years of experience working with startups, enterprises, and established brands, I've developed a methodology that balances strategic thinking with creative freedom — always in service of measurable results.

The name reflects the mission: Berlin — precision, structure, and European design excellence. C.O.C. (Creative Order of Craft) — the systematic, disciplined pursuit of creative perfection. Together: Zion Empire.
